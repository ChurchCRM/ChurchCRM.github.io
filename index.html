Features for Every Church

Dashboards
  • Dashboards gives you quick view about your members
Data Import
  • You can import data from a comma-separated value (.csv) file
Congregation Features
  • Group members into families
  • Upload family and members photo
Maps
  • Visualize members' home addresses with Google Maps integration
MailChimp
  • Integration with MailChimp lists
Calendar
  • Keep track of events and member birthdays
Finances
  • ChurchCRM has a finance function for tracking tithes, pledges and gifts
Reports
  • Email all families at once requesting info verification
  • Generate deposit slips
  • Generate end-of-year donation reports
Fundraiser
  • Fundraiser automation is used for events where members are buying and selling items and/or services to benefit the church
Volunteers
  • This feature is used to keep track of talent and the willingness of volunteers for specific tasks
Canvass Support
  • ChurchCRM includes comprehensive support to facilitate an every-member canvass effort
